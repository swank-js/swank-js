Adding a bookmarklet on iOS

It's kind of a pain to do this. Thanks to Chris Bray for figuring this out!

  1. Add this page as a bookmark
  2. Select and copy the bookmarklet text
  3. Go to your bookmarks and tap edit the one you just made
  4. Edit the name (if you like) and paste the URL.
  5. Profit!
